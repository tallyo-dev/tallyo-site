How It Works
Set the chores

Parents create chores and assign them to each kid. Takes 2 minutes. Use built-in templates or create your own.

Kids earn coins

Kids check off chores, earn XP and coins, level up, and unlock treasure chests. Every completion feels satisfying.

Redeem rewards

Kids spend coins on rewards you define — iPad time, movie night, ice cream, a trip to the park. Your rules, their motivation.
