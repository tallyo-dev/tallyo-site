Data Deletion Instructions

How to request deletion of your Tallyo account and associated personal data.

How To Request Deletion

If you have access to the app, open Settings > Account > Request Account Deletion and submit your request there.

If you cannot access the app, email support@tallyo.net from the email address associated with your account and include the subject line Account Deletion Request.

What Happens Next

We may need to verify your identity before processing the request. Deletion requests are typically completed within 30 days.

After processing, personal account information is deleted. Some group history may be anonymized instead of fully removed to preserve shared activity records.
