Support

Answers to common questions about Tallyo.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • When does Tallyo launch?
    Tallyo launches on April 13, 2026. It is already available for pre-order on the App Store, and pre-orders will download automatically on launch day.
  • How much does Tallyo cost?
    Tallyo is free to download and free to use.
  • What devices does Tallyo support?
    Tallyo works on iPhone and iPad running iOS 16 or later.
  • How do kids log in?
    Kids don't need an email address. Parents create the family and share a Family Code. Kids enter the code, pick their avatar, and they're in — no account, no email, no password.
  • Is Tallyo safe for kids?
    Yes. Tallyo collects minimal data for children — only a display name and avatar chosen by the parent. No ads, no behavioral tracking, no data sold to third parties. Tallyo is designed to be COPPA-compliant.
  • How do I contact support?
    Email us at support@tallyo.net. We usually respond within 24 hours.
