What Tallyo Can Do

A chore tracker kids actually open — and parents actually trust.

The Chore Board

A chore board they'll actually open.

One tap to check off. Kids see their whole week at a glance. The Win95-style weekly grid makes it satisfying — every checkmark earns XP and coins instantly.

Coins & Rewards

You define the rewards. Kids save up.

Create a reward catalog that fits your family — screen time, dessert, a movie night, a trip to the park. Kids can see exactly what they're working toward.

Levels & Quests

Every chore earns XP. Kids level up.

From Helper to Super Helper and beyond. Daily quests, streaks, and treasure chests keep kids motivated to come back every single day.

Parent Dashboard

Full control, at a glance.

See what's done, what's pending, and who's earning the most coins this week. Approve reward requests with one tap. You're always in charge.
