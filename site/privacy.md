## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address (required for authentication)
- Password (encrypted; we never have access to your plaintext password)
- Display name (visible to your group members)

**Profile Information:**
- Age confirmation (we record that you confirmed being 13 or older; we do not collect your date of birth)
- Terms of Service acceptance (version and timestamp)
- Privacy Policy acceptance (version and timestamp)
- Onboarding completion timestamp

**Habit and Task Data:**
- Task names you create
- Task categories (house, health, organization, wellbeing)
- Task difficulty settings (easy, medium, hard)
- Task recurrence settings (daily, times per week, free, per day cap)
- Task privacy preferences (public or private within group)
- Task completion logs (date, time, points earned)
- Task sort order preferences

**Group Data:**
- Group names you create
- Group settings you configure (theme, timezone, weekly reset day)
- Group invite tokens you generate or use

**Activity Data:**
- Reactions you give to other members' activity ("boa," "bora," fire)
- Account deletion requests and optional reason

### 2.2 Information Generated Automatically

**Derived Data:**
- Avatar initials (derived from your display name)
- Points, XP, and level calculations
- Streak data (consecutive active days)
- Weekly ranking position
- Variety count (categories with activity)

**Technical Data:**
- Device timezone (used for accurate day/week boundaries)
- App version and build number
- Session identifiers (randomly generated, not tied to device IDs)
- Performance metrics (operation timing for app optimization, stored locally)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about what we do not collect:

- Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
- Precise or approximate location data
- Contacts or address book
- Photos, camera, or microphone data
- Health or fitness data from HealthKit
- Calendar data
- Browsing history
- Data from other apps on your device
- Financial or payment information (no in-app purchases currently)
- Social media account information
- Date of birth (only 13+ confirmation)
- Real name (display name may be a nickname)
- Phone number

## 4. How Your Information Is Shared

### 4.1 Within Your Groups

When you join a group, the following information becomes visible to other group members:

| Data | Visibility |
|------|-----------|
| Display name | Visible to all group members |
| Avatar initials | Visible to all group members |
| Public task completions | Visible in activity feed |
| Points and XP earned | Visible in rankings |
| Current level | Visible in rankings |
| Weekly ranking position | Visible to all group members |
| Streak information | Visible in your stats |
| Reactions given | Visible on activity feed items |

**Private Task Protection:** Tasks you mark as "private" will NOT appear in the activity feed, will NOT count toward weekly group rankings, and their names will NOT be visible to other members. Private tasks only contribute to your personal XP and level.

### 4.2 Information NOT Shared with Group Members

The following information is never shared with other group members:

- Your email address
- Your password
- Your timezone (used internally for calculations only)
- Private task names and details
- Account deletion requests or reasons
- Your age confirmation status

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored on cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies that restrict data access to authorized users.

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

- All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- Passwords are hashed and salted using industry-standard algorithms (handled by Supabase Auth)
- Database access is controlled through Row Level Security (RLS) policies
- API authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with limited lifespans
- Session tokens are refreshed automatically and can be invalidated on sign-out

### 5.3 Security Limitations

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide you the Service:

|-----------|-----------------|
| Account information | Duration of account |

When you request account deletion:

- Upon completion, your personal data (email, display name, profile) is deleted

- Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained indefinitely

### 6.3 Legal Retention

We may retain certain information beyond deletion for:

- Compliance with legal obligations
- Resolution of disputes
- Enforcement of our agreements
- Prevention of fraud or abuse
